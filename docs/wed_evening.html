Wednesday evening

Since many ISMIR participants have asked for recommendations which places could be worth visiting on Wednesday evening, we have put together a small (and probably evolving) list of suggestions.

Jamaaladeen Tacuma

Jamaaladeen Tacuma will be performing at the Porgy&Bess club at 8:30 p.m.

Flex club

For those of you who like to dance and have party, we would recommend the Flex club, as it is known to have by far the best soundsystem in Vienna and always hosts excellent DJs.

Visit a Heuriger

If you want to intensify your knowledge of Austrian wines and food, you should visit a Heuriger. This place was suggested on the MIR mailing list.
