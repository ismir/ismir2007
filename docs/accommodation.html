Hotels

We have selected a number of two- to four-star hotels from which the conference venue can be reached within 15 minutes. The hotels are very well equipped and range from EUR 80 to EUR 230 for a single room.

Hotel reservation is possible directly after completing the registration form or individually (please note that special rates apply only if using one of these two links).

The map shows the conference venue and the location of the selected hotels. For an interactive version of the map, click on the image.

Hotel Europa
Austria Trend Hotel Europa Wien, the superior business class hotel with the best location at the well-known pedestrian and shopping mile, the "Kärntner Straße", in the very heart of the old city of Vienna. Best comfort will be guaranteed in each of the 158 rooms, providing timeless elegance and noble design.

Hotel Mercure Secession
Located directly in the historical City, by the side of one of the most impressing Art Deco Buildings of Europe, the “Wiener Secession”. State Opera, museums, the famous “Ringstraße”, St. Stephans Cathedrale and the new Opera House are in short walking distance.

Hotel Ananas
Next to a subway station that connects to the conference venue, the Hotel Ananas is a typical Viennese hotel.

Hotel Favorita
Close to the city centre the Hotel Favorita offers easy access to all the major places of interest. By underground U1 it is only 5 minutes to the city centre.

Hotel Erzherzog Rainer
Hotel Erzherzog Rainer is only a few minutes away from the State Opera, with good connections to the underground and trams.

Hotel Kaiserhof
The luxurious Kaiserhof hotel is just around the corner from the Vienna University of Technology, and is equipped with sauna, gym and steam bath.

Hotel Falkensteiner Palace
The luxurious Falkensteiner Palace Hotel is 2 minutes from a subway station, and offers a wide varity of wellness and recreational possibilities, and beautifully equipped rooms.

Hotel ibis Wien Schönbrunnerstraße
The Ibis Hotel recently opened and is close to Naschmarkt. The conference venue can be reached within 15 minutes by subway.

Hotel Atlas
Hotel Atlas offers very reasonably prices rooms and is within walking distance from the subway station U2. The Vienna University of Technology can be reached in 10 minutes. It is also very close to the city center with all of its historical attractions.

Hotel Clima City
The Clima Cityhotel is within walking distance (approx. 10 minutes) from the conference venue. It is also close to the beautiful Belvedere castle, a famous tourist attraction.

Hotel Theresianum
Situated between the famous streets Ringstrasse and Gürtel, the Hotel beim Theresianum is close to the U1 subway station that directly stops at Vienna University of Technology.
